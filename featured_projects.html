Automated Resume Scoring Service
for SuperJob

Every day recruiters get a lot of incoming resumes from the candidates. However, only some resumes are relevant for each particular job. As a result, recruiters waste their time processing irrelevant resumes. To address this problem, SuperJob decided to develop a feature that can help recruiters prioritize incoming resumes in their inboxes. We developed a ranking/scoring function to enable this feature.
