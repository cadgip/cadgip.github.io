Content Mining & Understanding

Robust solution for managing large-scale textual collections coming from diverse sources offline and in real-time.

What is it about?

Extract information from publicly available data sources like online reviews and social media discussions to understand and optimize what is being thought about your company and products. Crowd source your customers' feedback and conduct social market research. Identify the polarity of product reviews and discussions around the web. Classify documents into groups and extract topics.

Benefits

Drive rapid growth by putting publicly available data sources to work and identify new business opportunities to drive rapid growth in your organization.

Achieve high-quality results by leveraging machine learning and powerful text analytics to obtain high-quality results and insights.

Identify trends and uncover early-stage trends and opinions among your audience for the purpose of marketing and consumer research and enjoy a competitive edge.

Gather feedback from your customers about new product launches, political campaigns, and financial markets giving insights beyond those from legacy data.

Organize the documents in groups with our state-of-the-art flat and hierarchical classifiers based on Fasttext deep learning framework.

Identify hidden themes in your collection with large-scale topic modeling powered by Apache Spark.
